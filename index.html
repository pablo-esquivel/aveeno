WHAT'S YOUR TYPE?

Sunscreen Types

Mineral

Mineral sunscreens are ideal for sensitive skin. These kind of sunscreens form a shield over your skin to protect you from UV light.

Chemical

Chemical sunscreens absorb into the skin. They use the sun's rays and transform them into other forms of energy, like heat.

15 Min Before

Put on your sunscreen 15 minutes before getting under the sun, it takes some time for your skin to absorb it.

How Often?

Apply sunscreen every 2 hours of being in the sun to ensure maximum protection.
